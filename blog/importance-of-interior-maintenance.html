How Detail Tech Elevates Interiors

Lamborghini Huracan 610

Detail Tech recently serviced a Lamborghini Huracan 610 for an interior detail. As timeless as Lamborghini's interior is, every vehicle's interior features require the same care as its exterior.

Before the appointment, the owner of the Huracan expressed that the vehicle would endure more interior debris, as a result from the weather. He started to notice more dirt, dust, and environmental elements seeping their way into the interior's crevices. He planned for the Huracan 610 to be a long-term enjoyment and had future plans to redesign the exterior.

Our Level 1 Interior was the perfect solution to not only eliminate debris but also enhance its timeless features. Our Level 1 interior was all the Huracan needed to retain its distinctive and luxe features. By eliminating daily debris with a thorough steam of the interior panels and small crevices, pressure washed mats, application of odor neutralizer, and interior window polish - all interior aspects were refurbished.

Similarly, to the Huracan, the owner of a Jeep Wrangler was long overdue for interior maintenance and his goal was a complete interior revival. We recommended one of our unique packages, a Level 2 Interior. The process starts by the removal all debris and external contaminants with an intensive vacuum, steam, and sterilization of the paneling, and a shampoo & extraction of all carpeted surfaces to lift unwanted odors and remnants. With a level 2 interior, a leather conditioning treatment is applied as well as a protective sealant on all paneling. Applying a conditioner and protective sealant not only provides a brand-new looking interior, but also combats everyday elements on different surfaces. See the before and after results below!

Essential Service for Preservation

Our exclusive solutions, specifically a Level 1 interior and Ceramic Coat service, would be crucial to achieve long term interior and exterior protection. Since the exterior awaited its makeover, we serviced a ceramic glass coating to the windows and wheels. Making both surfaces dirt and corrosion resistant. With Detail Tech's exclusive ceramic application, glass surfaces are protected from micro scratching, yielding clearer visibility in any weather condition, and protecting each surfaces' hydrophobic properties for 2 full years. A ceramic wheel application will combat brake dust, corrosion and other environmental elements.
