The Value of Professional Ceramic Coating

Nov 2, 2023 2 min read

How It Works

Ceramic Coating is one of the most significant services available to various vehicles. A ceramic coating application involves two chemical compounds, graphene, and ceramic, which work simultaneously to bond to the vehicle's clear coat.

Graphene is a hydrophobic chemical compound that creates a beading effect when water contacts the vehicle. A graphene compound prevents exterior elements from adhering to the paint. In turn, ceramics coating achieves everything that graphene can do and provides a chemical bond to the clear coat ensuring the paint lasts its full 8-year lifespan.

Detail Tech's Step-by-Step Process

  1. Hand wash and dry service of full vehicle.
  2. Apply our iron removal and clay bar treatment to ensure the paint is spotless before applying the coating.
  3. Begin our 2-step paint correction on the entire vehicle to ensure the paint is smooth and swirl-free.
  4. Layer our coating prep to remove any residue that could have been left behind during the previous step.
  5. Administer our System X, 8-year ceramic coating to the entire vehicle.

When To Service Your Vehicle for A Ceramic Coating

The best time to get a ceramic coating is as soon as you purchase the vehicle. If the vehicle is brand new the paint is practically perfect, making it extremely vulnerable to exterior elements such as brake dust, iron particles, rust, etc. It is recommended you schedule a ceramic coating as soon as possible to preserve its excellent condition.

If you are thinking about trading in or selling, right now is a great time to schedule! Detail Tech is a certified ceramic coating installer.
