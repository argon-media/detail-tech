Rust: A Damaging Element and How Detail Tech Will Help Prevent It

Dec 20, 2023 2 min read

In a four-season climate, it is hard to manage the exterior vulnerabilities of your vehicle. At Detail Tech we understand the importance of exterior and paint protection. Below we share an extensive guide on how to contain the spread of rust.

Detail Tech's Most Popular Package for a Rust-Proof Vehicle

Our first recommendation to any client with the goal of rust prevention is our Level 2 Exterior package. This includes a full iron decontamination, clay bar, and our 303 Graphene Nano Spray Coating, a 7-month life-expectancy. Our iron decontamination process is an essential step to initiate the loosening of iron build-up before turning into rust. Once the iron decontamination is set, we start clay bar treatment which eliminates leftover iron build-up and all other corrosive elements. To preserve the perfect condition of the paint, our 303 Nano spray seals the paint to prevent rust, deterioration, and abrasions.

Detail Tech has an extensive process used to prevent rust. Our team will give their full attention to locate every spot of iron accumulation and then apply the proper removal compounds. This process can take upwards of half a day. Along with the precise application, we ensure the use of leading, industry-grade products to achieve the best results. The 303 Nano Spray is a graphene coating that prevents exterior elements from penetrating the vehicle's surface. Once fully bonded to the vehicle's surface, the coating will provide a 7-month hard layer protection. To maintain a healthy exterior, we recommend purchasing our Level 2 Exterior package. To maintain the healthy look of your vehicle, it should be applied every 3 months if it is parked or stored in a garage.
