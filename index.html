The story

Two guys, one shop,
zero corporate nonsense.

It started in a home garage in 2022 — detailing on the side until the work outgrew the space. The other owner was traveling the country wrapping fleets and commercial graphics. They met at the gym, started sharing a shop, and kept handing each other work until staying separate stopped making sense. So they merged.

Today Detail Tech runs PPF, wraps, tint, ceramic, correction and detailing out of one Crystal Lake shop — same two owners on the floor, doing the work and answering the phone. When you call, you get an owner. Not a call center. Not a service writer.

— The Owners
